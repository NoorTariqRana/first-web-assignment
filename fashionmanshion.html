FASHION

The power of a great outfit is impossible to overstate. At its best, fashion has the ability to transform your mood, identity, and, of course, your look. It can be fun, refreshing, and purposeful. But as with anything one does on a daily basis, it’s easy to fall into a fashion rut, feeling stale and drab no matter what you put on. If you have a closet full of clothes but not a single thing to wear, perhaps you just need some inspiration. We looked to some of the most iconic designers and couturiers for their thoughts on dressing in style.

WHITE & BLACK ARE ALWAYS IN,

WHITE

In summer it’s all about the white dress. A bright outfit, that works perfectly during the day and for the night. With a white dress in your wardrobe, you never make a mistake. If the style of the dress is not too special, you will keep it and wear it very often during the summer. I think it’s important to spend the money on classic pieces, which are wearable for a long time. Buy those clothes that are more timeless fashion pieces, I can wear them again and again – like most of my bags and my shoes. Let your personality shine through with accessories. Accessories are the part of the outfit that allows you to have fun with self-expression. If you are demure and lady-like, perhaps consider classic accessories like pearl earrings, a gold necklace, and nude sandals. If you are a little rock and roll, add in some leather elements. If you have a fun and colorful personality, let that shine through, as I did here. The beauty of white is in its simplicity. So, don’t overwhelm it with accessories. BLACK

There are many ways to style your black dress add a chunky necklace to a gallery openin or wear it plain for an evening with friends. Take this must-have dress from day to night, or casual to dressy with accessories alone. Need a chic look? Pair your dress with a hint of lace on the neckline. For a casual outing, wear your dress with sneakers and let your hair down. Add a baseball cap for a sporty look! To accentuate your waist. Add a metal belt along the smallest part. A belt transforms your look. Put on a motorcycle jacket and leather boots for the biker girl in you. This edgy look works for a casual evening out. For formal events, wear a statement necklace. A bold chunky necklace is an eye-catching piece for your outfit. Go easy on other accessories to keep the look refined. It's is a versatile color.

Few ideas whom you can't go wrong with:

  • All black dull? No way! A head-to-toe black outfit fits all styles; go for laidback-cool, and feminine. Try playing with contrasts between smooth and soft to add depth to your all black outfit.
  • Want to prevent your all black ensemble from looking too harsh? Soften your look by adding anthracite to the mix. This will add some nuance without compromising the overall dark feel.
  • Did you know that black and dark blue are in each other’s extension? Combining black with dark blue is stylish, chic and adds a classic feel.
  • Black suits all times ánd all styles - add a strong contemporary feel with a graphic print. A graphic touch can be added in different ways; from something as simple as a scarf to a cardigan.
  • Black is an ideal base to add cool, edgy detailing. Think zippers, studs, metal rings and distressed elements for a feminine look with rock-chic elegance.
  • You can’t go wrong with black and blue denim! The possibilities are endless and success is guaranteed – 100% fail proof.
